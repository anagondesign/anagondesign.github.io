A magazine that centers around life in New York City.

CONTENT

Left: An advertisement for the Museum of Ice Cream. Right: A page titled "noise", which features dialogue heard directly from the streets of New York.

The "Borough Bounce" section highlights different places that New Yorkers can travel to within each of the five boroughs in order to have fun, see new places, or eat new dishes. 

The word 'RISE' is stacked upon itself several times and also  increases in saturation in order to create a lifting effect.

Each dish is numbered in order from 1 to 5 and is labeled by name and location. Some fun facts about the dishes are also included as sidebars.

There are different spreads for different departments (celeb + fashion, home + health, and beauty + food) . Each section has a designated color and label.
