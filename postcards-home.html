POSTCARDS

Create 4 full color seasonal promotional postcards and one universal back design. All of them must be shown as a group with a consistent design throughout. 

SKETCHES

Preliminary sketches were done in my sketch book. I researched keywords online and brainstormed possible themes for the postcards. My four main ideas for themes were fantasy creatures, seasons, horror/halloween monsters, and holiday cards.

Based on early sketches, I zeroed in on one idea and developed a plan of consistency. I decided to develop my cards based on a thematic combination of Halloween and Valentines Day. My target audience for these would be young adults who want to send corny postcards to their loved ones.

FINAL

I finished the postcards using a limited color palette of red, black, and white. Anything colored in red was done using color pencil but for the drawings themselves, I used a combination of Micron pens and black waterproof India ink. 

The infamous Scream yelling "I miss you so much I want to scream." 

A zombie rising up from his grave with a bouquet of roses exclaiming "I was dead inside until I met you!"

A vampire woman smiling with her head tilted up as blood drips down her neck from a bite mark. The text reads "Do you believe in love at first BITE?"

A red-eyed kraken crying while holding onto a cracked heart. The words "My heart is kraken without you here"  are written in the background. 

The universal backside for the postcards. A small read heart is within each corner, all connect by a dark, jagged line. The card is divided in half by a vertical red line that leads up to a heart-eyed skull in the top center of the card.
